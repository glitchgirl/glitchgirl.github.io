Hey!
  My name is Morgan Smith and I am currently a software instructor at the clubhouse! I teach full-stack web development, including version control(Github), HTML, CSS (SASS), javascript (es6), node.js, express, and MySQL. I also teach soft skills like project management, SEO, accessibility, conflict resolution, and how to write documentation. In my free time, I enjoy reading and making art. With a background in e-commerce and DevOps, I bring many facets of development to my students so they leave my class as well rounded full-stack developers.


  My drive to teach is inspired by my desire to give back to my local community, and also to make the internet a better place through more inclusive design. I also love learning alongside my students to sharpen my skills, especially challenging myself to master skills that I think I am weaker in.
